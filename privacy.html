Chuck — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Chuck is built to keep your data on your device. This policy explains what happens to your information when you use the app.

What we store

Your habits, streaks, check-ins, and settings are stored locally on your device only. Chuck does not require an account, and we do not operate a server that stores your personal habit data.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device using Apple's notification system. We do not see or store the content of these notifications remotely.

Subscriptions & purchases

Chuck offers optional subscriptions processed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat, our subscription management provider. RevenueCat receives purchase and subscription status information (such as your anonymized purchase history and entitlement status) in order to unlock premium features and manage your subscription. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details on how they handle this data.

No third-party advertising or tracking

Chuck does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not sell or share your data for advertising purposes.
